Set up your projects

Gets your Behance work onto the site. About a minute.

1 Copy your Behance page

Open your Behance profile, scroll all the way to the bottom so every project loads, then press Ctrl+A and Ctrl+C (⌘A / ⌘C on Mac).

Paste it in the box below.

Didn't find everything?

Pasting a page sometimes drops the image links. For a complete grab: on your Behance profile press F12, click Console, paste the snippet below, press Enter, then drop the downloaded file here.

Drop the downloaded projects.json here

2 Tidy them up

Fix any titles, add a tag to each (tags become the filter buttons), and remove anything you don't want shown.

TitleTag

3 Download and drop in

Save projects.js into the same folder as index.html, then open index.html. That's it — no server, no setup.
